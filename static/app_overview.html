Application Overview


Just Enough will be an introductory language-learning tool. Users will be able to create an account that they can log in and out of to store their progress and preferred languages. Within the Web App, users will be able to select from a variety of languages, and they will also be able to apply filters to refine what kind of words they will learn--such as greetings, colors, numbers, common verbs, etc.

Users will have many ways of learning their selected language(s). There will be a flashcard feature--with one side in English and one in their desired language--that will allow users to learn and test their knowledge, and there will be a quiz feature that will randomly generate a 10 question quiz that asks users to match a word with its English translation.
